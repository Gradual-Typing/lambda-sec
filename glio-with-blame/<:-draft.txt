In order to state the blame-subtyping theorem, we need a subtyping relation <: to capture the notion of safe casts,
that is, a static approximation of casts that never result in blames.



+---------+
| 𝓁̂₁ ≃ 𝓁̂₂ |
+---------+

------
𝓁̂ ≃ ⋆

------
𝓁 ≃ 𝓁

  where 𝓁 ∈ ℒ . 𝓁̂ ∈ ℒ ∪ {⋆}



+----------+
| 𝓁̂₁ ≺: 𝓁̂₂ |
+----------+

------
𝓁̂ ≺: ⋆

𝓁₁ ≼ 𝓁₂
--------
𝓁₁ ≺: 𝓁₂

  where 𝓁₁ , 𝓁₂ ∈ ℒ . 𝓁̂ ∈ ℒ ∪ {⋆}



B ∷= Unit | Bool | Label

| S <: T |
+--------+
